Project Case Study

Project Overview

OffbeatExperience (branded as Obeat.) is an online reservation engine built specifically for discoverability and booking of unique, remote homestays, eco-farms, and hidden local stays in Siliguri, India, and the surrounding Himalayan foothill regions.

The platform is designed to provide travelers with an immersive look at local hospitality, village life, and nature, while equipping local hosts with simple reservation management portals.

The Challenge

Popular booking platforms are optimized for urban hotels and standardized tourist stays. They fail to convey the organic warmth, local meals, and remote scenic environments of rural cottages and mountain homestays.

The challenge lay in establishing a design system that feels nature-focused, highlights cultural immersion, and integrates a highly responsive booking search and registration engine that operates smoothly on varying regional connection speeds.

Design & Development Strategy

We built Obeat. with visual exploration and simplified user travel funnels in mind:

Scenic Imagery & Vibe Alignment: Centered the theme around high-fidelity mountain imagery. Set up a primary green accent scheme to reflect nature, forests, and organic travel.

Intuitive Search Booking Widget: Created a custom search panel in the hero area ("Where to?", "Check in", "Check out") allowing users to find specific villages or choose a stay based on local highlights (e.g. tea gardens, mountain farms).

Supply Acquisition & Onboarding: Integrated host-acquisition banners ("Become a Host") to capture homestay registrations, complete with secure account login modules for tracking travelers and payouts.
