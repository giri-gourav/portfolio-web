Project Case Study

Project Overview

Brickell Perio is an elite periodontal and dental implant practice operating in Miami, Florida under Dr. Irene Marron-Tarrazzi. The clinic needed a fully customized digital experience that could elevate patient inquiries and project premium board-certified expertise in a competitive metropolitan area.

We worked to replace their legacy website with a high-performance web platform that builds trust, communicates advanced periodontal solutions, and streamlines the patient appointment funnel.

The Challenge

Medical and dental websites frequently suffer from looking generic, overly clinical, or visually cold. This often causes friction for patients seeking specialized care who may already feel anxious about complex periodontal procedures.

The challenge was to project clinical authority and board-certified credibility (e.g. Diplomate status, advanced procedures) while maintaining a warm, welcoming, and high-end aesthetic that encourages direct patient outreach and booking.

Design & Development Strategy

Our approach combined professional typography, custom layout spacing, and a trust-based hierarchy:

Trust Indicators: Highlighted Dr. Marron-Tarrazzi’s elite background as a Diplomate of the American Board of Periodontology. Prominently featured practice stats (20+ years of experience, 5k+ smiles restored) to build immediate authority.

Modern UI Flow: Used a soft teal color scheme, custom circular image modules, and a layout that segments complex periodontal services into easy-to-understand pathways.

Conversion-Driven UX: Integrated persistent, clean call-to-actions ("Book Appointment", "(305) 646-1524") and structured quick-contact pathways for both local emergency requests and consultation scheduling.
